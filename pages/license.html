MAI Dataset

MAI is an under-development benchmark for behavioral alignment drift scored with PCPI. It is being built toward a target corpus of 20,000+ human-annotated examples from structured test cases, stress tests, and model-response evaluations. Early versions are released in staged benchmark sets. Research access may be available under a Data Use Agreement as the dataset matures.
